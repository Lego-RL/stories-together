# Application Ideas
- Character or word limit on contributions to individual stories. Per project or set limit across all stories?
- Users "reserve" contributing to the next part of the story & reservation lasts for period of time like 6-12 hours?
- Multiple people may submit follow-up passages, and others have a period of time to vote on the best passage to continue the story?

## Other interesting ideas
- The "Exquisite Corpse" Mode: Borrowing from the surrealist game, contributors only see the last sentence of the previous passage. This leads to hilarious, disjointed, and creative narratives.

- Genre Challenges: Implement a "Daily Prompt" or "Genre Locked" room where users must use specific keywords or themes to earn badges.

- Story Forking: Allow users to "fork" a story at a specific passage (similar to GitHub) to take the narrative in a completely different direction if they didn't like where the previous contributor went.
