# Project Design Document: Stories Together

## 1. Vision & Goals
*   **Purpose:** A collaborative digital environment for storytellers to share, co-create, and explore diverse narratives.
*   **Target Audience:** Writers, collaborators, and avid readers looking for a community-driven storytelling experience.
*   **Key User Goals:**
    1.  Discover and read featured collaborative stories.
    2.  Sign in to contribute to ongoing stories or start new ones.
    3.  Engage with a community of collaborators in a shared creative space.

## 2. Visual Identity
*   **Brand Personality:** Dark, Atmospheric, Minimalist, and Focused.
*   **Color Palette:**
    *   Primary (Background): #000000 (Pure Black)
    *   Secondary (Text/UI): #FFFFFF (White) and #888888 (Muted Gray)
    *   Accent: #F38D16 (Vibrant Orange - used for "Log In" and headers)
*   **Typography:**
    *   Headings: Modern Sans-Serif (e.g., Inter, Roboto) - Bold and Uppercase for section titles.
    *   Body: Clean Sans-Serif for readability against dark backgrounds.

## 3. Information Architecture (Key Screens)
*   **Home/Landing Page:** Hero section with branding, "Featured Stories" feed, and a clear "Log In" CTA.
*   **Story Catalog:** A searchable and filterable list of all collaborative stories.
*   **Story Reader/Editor:** A focused view for reading stories with integrated tools for adding contributions.
*   **Collaborator Profile:** Showcases a user's contributions, followed stories, and creative stats.

## 4. Design Principles
*   **Atmospheric Focus:** Use a dark theme to minimize distractions and keep focus on the content.
*   **Collaborative Clarity:** Clearly distinguish between different contributors' parts of a story.
*   **Community First:** Make actions like "Read Story" and "Collaborate" prominent and intuitive.
